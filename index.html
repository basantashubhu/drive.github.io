Programs

in-class instruction - Run on a continuous basis

30 hours of classroom instruction cost $ 205. You may start anytime – Tuesday through Friday (from5:00p.m. until 7:00p.m.) The Course shall be completed within 4 months from the date of enrollment.

We also offer: Mandatory Eight Hours of Safe Driving Practices at $ 150, and a Confidence Test at $ 55 (one time – 2hrs – in classroom session).

School reserves the right to cancel classes or driving lessons due to weather condition or other emergencies. (broken car, etc.). No refund for classroom hours. All prices are subject to change.

behind-the-wheel instruction - Done 7days a week (8a.m.-9p.m.)

One hour of driving cost $ 55.00. Road appointments will be scheduled in the order in which students sign up. Home pick-up services are provided unless other arrangements are made . Car rental for Road Test is available with mandatory two hours evaluation ($110) prior to EACH test.

There will be a $ 55 refundable security deposit required. A 24 HOUR notice MUST be given to cancel a road lesson. Otherwise there will be a $ 55 charge for every missed one-hour appointment.

teen's - package

The cost for a “30/8″ hrs Program – 30 hrs of Classroom ( including 8 hrs of Safe Driving Practices) and 8 hrs of Behind – the – Wheel instruction is $ 545. No refund after 7 days from thedate of enrollment. Program must be completed within 4 months.
