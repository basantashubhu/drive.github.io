payment policy

Course may be paid preferably in full,or in two installments. The first payment of $ 275is due upon enrollment. The second payment of $ 270 is due by the end of the second week after enrollment.There will be a $ 25 fee for all checks returned for any reason.

DMV lincense exam

Test service fee (“pass or fail”) to Atlas Driving School starts at $ 90. ( + travel and + waiting time). In the event of cancellations (less than 48 hrs prior to the exam), or “no show” on test date, all exam fee will be forfeite
